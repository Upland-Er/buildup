GSBF Rules

3 Strike 1 day system

If you break one of the following rules you will lose 1 strike, if you lose all 3 strikes you will be banned from buildup the next day, if it is a Friday then you will be punished the following Monday

No targetting

A tag or chase is designated targetting if when it begins the person tagged or being chased was further away from those who initiated the chase then another person.

If you are caught you must put effort into to your chases

This rule is rather self explanatory, if you are seen simply walking while you are caught you will be required to speak to the council.

Pausing is only permitted if you are injured

A stitch does not count as an injury, you are permitted to pause if you slipped, fell or tripped.
